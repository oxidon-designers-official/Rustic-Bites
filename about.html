The history of Rustic Bites in the world

Founded by Thresiamma James, Rustic Bites began its journey in 2014 as a small home-based venture driven by a passion for authentic flavours. What started in a kitchen has today grown into a brand rooted in tradition and shaped by purpose. As part of our brand evolution and growth, we are now proudly known as Rustic Bites.

At Rustic Bites, we blend time-honoured recipes with mindful practices to create snacks that satisfy cravings while keeping health in focus. Our journey continues with the same dedication, care, and love for good food — just with a stronger vision and a bolder identity.
